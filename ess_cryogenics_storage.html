Cryoline + WPS PIDHelium recovery line is vacuum-insulated; no conditioning valves on Cryoline
Alignment strategy Positioning vs. virtual interface points (center plane of QM & QVB) with ±1 mm required accuracy. Coarse on supports: ±45 mm (V), ±35 mm (H). Fine: 1 mm/turn all directions.
Commissioning steps Cleaning/conditioning → Leak tests of control valve seats → Cool-down → Surface temperature (cold spots) → Instrumentation tests at cryo T → 8 K heat-load measurements → Warm-up.
Lessons learned Endoscopic cleanliness checks of process pipes & control valves before installation; verify valve seat leak-tightness; convection breakers on larger valve inserts.
